Puppy Concierge

From the moment your new puppy joins your family, we're here to help make the transition smooth and joyful. Our Puppy Concierge Service offers personalized guidance, home visits, basic training foundations, potty training support, and answers to all your new-puppy questions. We help set your pup up for success and give you peace of mind during those exciting (and sometimes overwhelming) first months.
